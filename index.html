Explore the Path to Licensure

This interactive map of the United States visualizes architecture schools by program length and exam division performance. Compare institutions, uncover trends, and engage with the evolving standards of architectural licensure.

Education

Architecture programs vary in length from 5 to 8 years. A 5-year accredited undergraduate program leads directly to licensure eligibility, while a 4-year pre-professional degree typically requires an additional 1 to 3 years of graduate study to meet licensure requirements.

Examination

The map displays pass rates by institution for each of the six Architect Registration Examination (ARE) divisions: Practice Management, Project Management, Programming & Analysis, Project Planning & Design, Project Development & Documentation, and Construction & Evaluation.
